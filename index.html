Export

Batch export your selected recipes in several formats, send to friends and family, or create a backup of your recipes so you’re never tied to one system:

• MiNoms file: perfect for sending to someone who has the app, or making a backup for yourself that can be imported back into the app.

• HTML file: can be opened on any platform, perfect for creating a recipe book or sending files to anyone who doesn’t have the app

• JSON file: a standard data file type that conforms to the Google recipe format and cen be easily converted to CSV

• Unformatted text: basic, no nonsense text.

Shopping List

Send your recipe ingredients to Apple’s Reminders app, the MiNoms shopping list, or to the pasteboard to be copied to whichever list app you’re using, that way when you go to the store you’re sure to pick up everything you need to make your recipes!
