Privacy Policy

MiNoms places a high emphasis on privacy and does not collect any of your personal information unless you voluntarily provide it through contact with our support team.

Unfortunately many apps claim to respect your privacy, but in reality they collect, and sometimes even sell, your personal information anyway. We understand the importance of knowing who is collecting and using your personal data, and encourage our users to use privacy verification tools such as Lockdown Privacy to confirm our commitment to user privacy.

In the event that you do choose to contact us, any information you provide, such as your name and email address, will only be used to assist you and will not be shared with any third parties.
